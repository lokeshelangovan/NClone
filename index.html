Money Heist

"Money Heist" is a Spanish crime thriller that follows a group of skilled robbers, led by the enigmatic Professor, as they execute meticulously planned heists on major institutions like the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain. The series weaves intense action, personal drama, and high-stakes tension as the team faces internal conflicts and external threats.
